Equation + Sessions == A Match Made in Education

Since launching Predicate, we frequently encounter two cases that stun the technical development of an aspiring innovator - discouragement and plateaus. First and foremost, there is a constant battle against discouragement. We see this regularly in our seasonal classes - and it comes by way of the often spoken line “it’s not working!”. Learning to code is in many cases learning from mistakes. The key to keeping discouragement at bay is to quickly identify, correct, and shape a learning opportunity around a misstep. The longer a problem festers, the greater the amount of discouragement. Second, we often see our innovators falling into a plateau - they build up just enough skills to get comfortable and don’t seek beyond their comfort zone (in many cases for fear of discouragement). Plateaus lead to boredom. Learning technology is very much a lifelong journey as the field is constantly changing. Good technologists are “fit for change” and we need to ensure the same for our children.

In addition to the Equation Bundle, we’re really excited to announce Predicate Sessions, a new series of 90-minute / $40 “drop in” classes that will focus on a variety of general computing and coding topics. While the Equation Bundle offers the seed for creating an innovator, we recognize that hardware and self study alone may not work for everyone. Our Sessions and 10-week Foundations classes are a very effective way to cultivate the learning process. Sessions are great for those who cannot commit to weekly classes but are still interested in learning more about the field and then go home and practice what they’ve learned. Sessions will keep the Equation Bundle primed with the latest software and reset (as needed) conditions back to factory default settings. Don’t worry parents! Help is just a session away!

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it possible to ship an Equation Bundle to my home?
Unfortunately, at the present time, the Equation Bundle is only available for pickup at our studio in Logan Twp, NJ.

Are we eligible to purchase a bundle if we are not presently enrolled at Predicate?
Anyone is eligible to purchase an Equation Bundle. Session availability is offered on a first come, first served basis.

What is the suggested age for Equation Bundle?
Our suggested minimum age for assembly and participation in the first session is 8.

Can adults buy the Equation Bundle?
Absolutely. Coding is not just great for kids :)

If we purchase a bundle do we need to sign up for any sessions?
Participation in the sessions are completely optional. You can sign up for as many as your schedule permits or none at all.

What is the cost of a 90-minute session?
The first 90-minute session is included in the cost of the Equation Bundle. Each additional session is priced at $40. Some sessions may introduce hardware that will be priced in addition to the $40 session.

Does the Equation Bundle provide everything that my child needs to learn how to code?
Yes! The Equation Bundle is ready to go. All you need is an Internet connection (wired or wifi).

If we purchase a bundle and want to take other classes are we still required to enroll in Predicate’s Bootcamp program?
Yes! Completion of Predicate’s Foundational Bootcamp program is still a requirement for any other 10-week seasonal program we offer at the academy.

How does the Equation + Session program differ from Predicate’s 10-week seasonal courses?
Sessions are “single shot” 90-minute classes focused around exploring the potential of the field. We will tackle a number of concepts - learning how to use and maintain Raspbian operating system, installing new software, converting the kit into gaming console, among others. There will be little overlap with our 10-week seasonal courses that focus on providing depth of instruction on a particular topic. It is possible to participate in either or both the seasonal courses as well as the sessions.

We already purchased a Predicate hardware kit for the Bootcamp program, do we need to purchase the Equation Bundle?
The Equation Bundle is primarily intended for use at home and participation in the sessions program. Due to the mounting and wiring of the $35 Raspberry Pi hardware, it is not easily suited for both in home and class options.

Can the Equation Bundle be used for any of Predicate’s seasonal courses?
Some programs require a special mounting of the Pi hardware and leaving the equipment in studio for the duration of the class. We strongly recommend leaving the Equation Bundle intact and separate from use during our seasonal courses.

How does the Equation Bundle compare with Kano and other Raspberry Pi kits?
Predicate Academy sells hardware at cost and we pride ourselves on our instructional experience. Our Equation Bundle is often the same hardware as other kits, but offered at a substantial discount. We recognize that our aspiring innovators thrive when good technology is combined with good instruction. Our focus is providing good technology at cost and differentiating ourselves by providing good instruction.
