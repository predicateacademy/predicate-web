Predicate Jumpstart

an onramp to innovation awesomeness

Predicate's Jumpstart class is a foundational experience in Computer Science for those who are interested in learning about the power of writing coding or desiring to enroll in our seasonal program. Our Jumpstart classes meets for a total of three hours, typically divided over two days. During this time we cover the basics of working with the Raspberry Pi micro-computer, using the Linux operating system, and writing code in Python. All of these concepts are wrapped around mini-projects that ignite creative thinking.

Here at Predicate, we teach with the tools of the trade and it starts with the Linux operating system. We love Linux! Predicate Academy teaches Linux, as opposed to Mac or Windows, due to its accessibility and wide adoption in the computing field. Linux powers most of the Internet. We write code using the Python programming language. Youth as early as 9 years old are easily able to grasp this programming language and a recent Wall Street Journal article confirms our position.

How it works...

1) Register for a Jumpstart class. We offer Jumpstart classes throughout the year outside of our seasonal class schedule. Jumpstart classes cost $89 and are typically split over two days, with 90 minutes of instructional time each class. Click here to view our current Jumpstart offerings. No experience is necessary to register and we provide all the hardware for each class.

2) Decide. Our classes are designed to spark conversation. How does your child respond to coding? We recognize learning to code is new to most of our student population and it's not for everyone. Our Jumpstart classes provide a nice way for your aspiring innovator to kick the coding tires and see if they like it.

3) Buy the Predi-Kit Hardware Bundle. The Predi-Kit includes a Raspberry Pi 3 - Model B, a case for the Pi, USB power adapter, 8GB microSDHC card preloaded with Raspbian and Predicate software, two full size breadboards, Pi Cobbler Plus (connects Pi to breadboard), eight 1K resistors, eight LEDs (blue, yellow, green, red), four large tact switches, and male/male jumper cables. The Predi-Kit costs $115 and is used in all classes that we teach. You can even use it as your computer at home! Click here to purchase the kit and we'll will reach out to schedule a time to assemble everything.

4) Enroll in a Seasonal Class. With the Jumpstart coding primer complete, your child is welcome to enroll in any of our seasonal classes. Keep in mind that our programs, as well as the computing field, are always changing. As a result, if your child misses more than three consecutive seasons, they will need to re-enroll in a Jumpstart class prior to participating in our Seasonal program.
